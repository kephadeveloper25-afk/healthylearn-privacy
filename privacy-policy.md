Health Learn Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 8, 2026
Data Collection and Storage
Health Learn stores information locally on your device to provide a personalized experience. This includes tips you have viewed, tips marked as favorites, tips marked as completed, achievement progress, and app settings and preferences.
No personal information is collected. All data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.
No External Data Sharing
Health Learn does not share, sell, or transmit any data to external services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or third parties. There are no analytics SDKs or tracking code in the app.
Local Storage Only
The app uses React Native AsyncStorage to store data locally. This data is accessible only to the app, is deleted when you uninstall the app, and can be cleared in your device's app settings.
Optional Notifications
You can enable daily health tip notifications. Notification preferences are stored locally and can be disabled at any time in app settings.
Children's Privacy
Health Learn is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
