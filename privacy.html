Health Learn Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

1. Data Collection and Storage

Health Learn stores information locally on your device to provide a personalized experience. This includes:

  • Health tips you've viewed
  • Tips marked as favorites
  • Tips marked as completed
  • Achievement progress and unlocked badges
  • App settings and notification preferences
  • Usage streak information

No personal information is collected. All data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

2. No External Data Sharing

Health Learn does not share, sell, or transmit any data to external services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or third parties. The app contains no analytics SDKs, tracking code, or advertising libraries.

3. Local Storage Only

The app uses React Native AsyncStorage to store data locally on your device. This data:

  • Is accessible only to the Health Learn app
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be manually cleared through your device's app settings
  • Never leaves your device

4. Optional Notifications

You can enable daily health tip notifications. Notification preferences are stored locally and can be disabled at any time in the app's settings. We do not send promotional or marketing notifications.

5. Children's Privacy

Health Learn is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app provides general health education content suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
